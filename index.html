Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers. If you have more questions, feel free to reach out – we’re happy to help.

Do you provide both hardware and networking services? +
Yes. We handle complete IT infrastructure – desktops, laptops, servers, routers, switches, cabling, Wi-Fi, firewalls, CCTV and more. You don’t have to coordinate with multiple vendors.
Can you support us remotely if we are in another city? +
Most issues can be resolved remotely through secure remote-access tools, phone or video calls. For hardware or cabling work, we can plan onsite visits as needed.
Do you offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC)? +
Yes. AMC plans include scheduled health checks, priority support, preventive maintenance and discounted onsite visits. We can customize a plan based on your device count and locations.
What information should I share to get a quotation? +
Just share your current setup (approx. number of systems, floors, rooms) and what you want to achieve (new office, upgrade Wi-Fi, secure network, etc.). Add any preferred brands or special requirements – we’ll take it from there.
